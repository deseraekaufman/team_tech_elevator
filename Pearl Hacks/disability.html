DISABILITY TECH

Thriving in Tech as a Disabled Woman

As a disabled women in tech and one of the founders of this webpage, I have noticed a huge disparity of resources for persons with disabilities in the tech field. The pandemic has changed tech industry dramatically, creating many accessibility friendly jobs. It is our hope that we will see a shift in company cultures, and that inclusive hiring will become a common practice.

Remember to always advocate for yourself.

You are not obligated to disclose the details of your disability.

Your disability does not make you less worthy!
