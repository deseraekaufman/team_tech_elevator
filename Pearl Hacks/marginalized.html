What About Me Though?

Have you been down the google rabbit hole trying to find resources for someone like you and you found this site and were disappointed to see that you were not included? Have you looked for inclusive resources only to find that other people's version of inclusive does not include you?

Well we want to help and we want your voice to be heard. Please join our team and submit content so others can hear your valuable voice and insights.

We want to make sure everyone is included and acknowledged here. Your voice is valuable and what you contribute to tech is needed.

In the meantime, please check out the following resources. If you're not seeing yourself represented, then whatever we can do to help you be the change you want to see, we're here to help.
